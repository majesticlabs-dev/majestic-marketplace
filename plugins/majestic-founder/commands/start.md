# Founder Toolkit

Five thinking frameworks for founders who need clarity, not more advice.

## Skill Registry

| Skill | What It Does |
|-------|-------------|
| `founder-mode` | Run your company with founder's direct involvement — contrarian principles from successful founders |
| `challenge-mode` | Aggressive intellectual challenge — identifies blind spots, calls out avoidance patterns |
| `pareto` | Find the vital 20% of actions producing 80% of results |
| `calendar-optimization` | Balance 4 types of productive time: Management, Creation, Consumption, Ideation |
| `rookie-mistakes` | Industry-specific pitfalls that kill new businesses, with prevention strategies |

## Step 1: What Do You Need?

Use `AskUserQuestion`:

"Which thinking framework do you need?"

Options:
1. **Founder Mode** — Challenge conventional management advice with contrarian founder principles
2. **Challenge Mode** — Get brutally honest feedback on your ideas and blind spots
3. **Pareto Analysis** — Find the 20% of actions that drive 80% of results
4. **Calendar Optimization** — Rebalance your time across the 4 types of productive work
5. **Rookie Mistakes** — Learn industry-specific pitfalls that kill new businesses
