# Follow-Up Email Templates

## Post-Discovery Next Steps Email

Send within 24 hours of discovery call:

```
Subject: Next steps for {{company}}

Hi {{first_name}},

Thanks for the conversation today. Based on what we discussed:

**What I heard:**
- [Pain point 1] is costing you [impact]
- [Stakeholder X] needs to weigh in on [concern]
- Timeline goal: [date/quarter]

**Proposed next steps:**
1. [Action 1] - Me - [Date]
2. [Action 2] - You - [Date]
3. [Action 3] - Both - [Date]

Does this align with what you're thinking? Reply with any adjustments.

[Your name]
```

## Investment Framing Template

For price objection handling:

```
"Based on what you've shared:
- [Problem] is currently costing you $[X] per [month/year]
- Our solution eliminates [Y]% of that cost
- That's $[savings] in your pocket within [timeframe]
- The investment is $[price], which pays for itself in [X] months

What matters more to you - the upfront investment or the ongoing return?"
```

## Shadow Selling - Internal Pitch Framework

Provide to your champion for internal presentations:

```
**Opening:**
"I've been evaluating [Product] to solve [our problem]. Here's what I found."

**Frame it as risk-free:**
"We can start with a [pilot/trial] to prove value before full commitment."

**If asked about cost:**
"The investment is $[X], which pays for itself in [Y] months based on [our current cost of the problem]."

**If asked about implementation:**
"Implementation takes [timeframe] with [minimal disruption to team]."

**If they compare to [Competitor]:**
"I evaluated [Competitor]. They fall short on [gap], which is critical for us."

**Close with data:**
"[Similar company in our industry] saw [result] in [timeframe]. I believe we can achieve similar results."
```

## Champion Support Materials

After Shadow Selling setup, provide:

1. **ROI Calculator:** Pre-filled with their numbers
2. **One-page summary:** Executive-friendly overview
3. **Comparison chart:** You vs. competitors on their key criteria
4. **Reference contacts:** Similar companies they can call
5. **FAQ document:** Anticipated questions with answers
