# Competitive Battle Cards

## Competitor Gap Analysis

**Discovery Question:**
In discovery calls, ask: "What's one thing your current provider falls short on?"

**Follow-Up Framework:**

1. **Quantify the impact:**
   "How much is that costing you in [time/money/opportunity]?"

2. **Position your strength:**
   "That's exactly what we built [Product] to solve. Let me show you how we handle [their gap]."

3. **Summarize with 3-bullet competitive edge:**

   "Here's how we're different from [Competitor]:
   - **[Gap they mentioned]:** We [your approach] vs. their [their limitation]
   - **[Additional differentiator 1]:** [Specific capability]
   - **[Additional differentiator 2]:** [Specific capability]"

**Example:**

Discovery: "What's one thing your current provider falls short on?"
Prospect: "Their reporting is really limited. We can't customize dashboards."

Response: "How much time does your team spend manually creating reports each week?"
Prospect: "Probably 10 hours across the team."

Close: "That's exactly why we built our reporting engine. Here's how we're different from [Competitor]:
- **Custom dashboards:** Drag-and-drop builder vs. their fixed templates
- **Real-time data:** Live updates vs. their 24-hour lag
- **Export flexibility:** Any format vs. CSV only

That 10 hours goes back to your team immediately."

### When We Win Against Them
- [Scenario 1]: They choose us because [reason]
- [Scenario 2]: We win when [condition]
- [Scenario 3]: Our advantage is [feature/capability]

### When We Lose Against Them
- [Scenario 1]: They choose [Competitor] because [reason]
- [Scenario 2]: We lose when [condition]
- **How to counter:** [Strategy]

### Positioning Statement
"[Competitor] is a great choice if you need [their strength]. We're different because we focus on [our strength], which means [benefit to customer]. Most companies choose us when [ideal scenario]."

### Landmines to Plant
Questions that expose competitor weakness:
- "How do they handle [thing they do poorly]?"
- "Ask them about [known issue]."
- "What happens when you need [capability they lack]?"

### Objection Handling
**"[Competitor] is cheaper"**
"You're right - and here's why. [Competitor] doesn't include [X, Y, Z]. When you add those, the total cost is actually [comparison]."

**"[Competitor] has feature X"**
"True - and here's why we built it differently. [Explanation of our approach and why it's better for them]."

### Customer Proof
[Similar company] evaluated [Competitor] and chose us because [reason]. Quote: "[direct quote about why they chose us]"

### Do NOT Say
- [Negative statement that backfires]
- [Claim we can't substantiate]
- [Anything that acknowledges competitor strength unnecessarily]
