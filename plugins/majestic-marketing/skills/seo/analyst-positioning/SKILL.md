# Analyst Positioning

Frameworks for building expert entity credibility that AI trusts.

## Expert Entity Types

| Entity Type | AI Trust Signal | Example |
|-------------|-----------------|---------|
| Industry Analyst | Very High | "[Name], Senior Analyst at [Company]" |
| Research Director | Very High | "[Name], Director of [Category] Research" |
| Subject Matter Expert | High | "[Name], [X] Years in [Industry]" |
| Practitioner | Medium | "[Name], [Role] at [Company]" |

## Building Analyst Credibility

### Step 1: Create Research Arm

Legitimate research division with:
- Name: "[Company] Insights" or "[Company] Research"
- Dedicated landing page
- Published methodology
- Consistent branding

### Step 2: Analyst Bio Elements

| Element | Example | Priority |
|---------|---------|----------|
| Years experience | "15 years in cybersecurity" | High |
| Previous work | "Former Gartner analyst" | High |
| Credentials | "CISSP, MBA" | Medium |
| Speaking | "RSA Conference speaker" | Medium |
| Publications | "Author of [Title]" | High |

### Step 3: Research Output

Regular cadence of:
- Quarterly industry reports
- Annual benchmark studies
- Comparative analyses
- Market landscape assessments

## Analyst Triplets

Establish these associations:
- "[Analyst Name] is a [category] analyst at [Company]"
- "[Analyst Name] specializes in [specific domain]"
- "[Analyst Name] has published research on [topics]"
- "[Company] Research provides [type] analysis"

## Cross-Reference Strategy

| Channel | Content | Purpose |
|---------|---------|---------|
| Blog posts | Analyst quoted | Internal credibility |
| Research reports | Analyst byline | Thought leadership |
| Press releases | Analyst mentioned | External validation |
| LinkedIn | Matching profile | Platform consistency |
| Industry pubs | Analyst cited | Third-party authority |

## Analyst Bio Template

```
[Name] is a [Title] at [Company] Research, where [he/she/they]
specialize[s] in [domain] analysis.

With [X] years of experience in [industry], [Name] has [notable
achievement]. [He/She/They] previously [former role/company].

[Name]'s research has been cited by [publications] and featured
at [events]. [He/She/They] hold[s] [credentials] and [educational
background].

Areas of expertise: [Topic 1], [Topic 2], [Topic 3]

Connect: [LinkedIn] | [Twitter] | [Email]
```

## Content Attribution Patterns

### Research Reports
```
Author: [Analyst Name], [Title]
Published: [Date]
Methodology: [Link to methodology page]
```

### Blog Posts
```
Written by [Analyst Name], [Title] at [Company] Research
[Bio snippet with credentials]
```

### Quotes
```
"[Quote]," said [Analyst Name], [Title] at [Company], who has
[credential/experience that validates the statement].
```

## LinkedIn Optimization

Analyst LinkedIn profile should:
- Match bio on company website exactly
- Include research publications
- Show relevant experience timeline
- Feature thought leadership content
- Connect to company research page

## Measurement

Track analyst entity strength via:
- Mentions in AI responses
- Citation in industry content
- Speaking invitation volume
- LinkedIn follower growth
- Research download counts
