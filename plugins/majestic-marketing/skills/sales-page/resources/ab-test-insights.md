# A/B Test Insights

Data-backed findings from sales page testing.

## Headlines

| Element Tested | Finding | Lift | Notes |
|----------------|---------|------|-------|
| Benefit vs Problem focus | Problem-focused headlines often outperform | +10-30% | Works best for problem-aware audiences |
| With number vs Without | Specific numbers increase credibility | +15-25% | "Save 4.5 hours" beats "Save hours" |
| Question vs Statement | Questions perform well early in awareness | +5-20% | Statements better for solution-aware |
| Personalization (name) | Limited impact on cold traffic | +0-5% | Higher impact on warm traffic |

## Social Proof

| Element Tested | Finding | Lift | Notes |
|----------------|---------|------|-------|
| Testimonial placement | Above fold increases trust signals | +5-15% | Must be relevant to promise |
| Photo vs No photo | Real photos outperform stock | +10-20% | Authentic > polished |
| Video vs Text | Video increases engagement | +20-40% | But takes longer to consume |
| Specific results | "$47,329" beats "significant increase" | +15-30% | Specificity = credibility |

## CTAs

| Element Tested | Finding | Lift | Notes |
|----------------|---------|------|-------|
| Button color | Contrast matters more than specific color | +5-20% | Must stand out from page |
| Button copy | Benefit-oriented beats command | +10-25% | "Get instant access" > "Submit" |
| Single vs Multiple | Multiple CTAs throughout page | +15-30% | Don't make them scroll to act |
| CTA size | Larger buttons get more clicks | +5-15% | But don't overwhelm the design |

## Page Structure

| Element Tested | Finding | Lift | Notes |
|----------------|---------|------|-------|
| Long vs Short | Long-form wins for high-consideration purchases | +20-50% | Short for low-friction offers |
| Video above fold | Can increase or decrease - test | ±20% | Depends on video quality |
| FAQ section | Reduces support tickets, slight conversion lift | +3-8% | Address real objections |
| Guarantee prominence | Bold guarantees increase conversions | +10-20% | Place near CTA |

## Price Presentation

| Element Tested | Finding | Lift | Notes |
|----------------|---------|------|-------|
| Value anchoring | Showing "real value" first increases perceived value | +15-30% | Must be believable |
| Payment plans | Offering payments increases conversion | +20-40% | Especially for >$100 products |
| Price per day | Daily breakdown seems smaller | +5-15% | "Just $1.50/day" |
| Strikethrough pricing | Can work, but risks looking "salesy" | ±10% | Use authentically |
