# Email Copy Template

Use this template structure for every email in a nurture sequence.

## Full Email Template

```markdown
## EMAIL [#]: [Name]

**Send Timing:** Day [X] after [trigger]
**Goal:** [Specific goal]

### Subject Lines (Test These)
1. "[Option 1]"
2. "[Option 2]"
3. "[Option 3]"

### Preview Text
"[Preview text that complements subject]"

### Email Structure

**Opening Hook:**
[2-3 sentences that grab attention and reference their situation]

**Body:**
[Main content - story, value, or teaching point]
[Keep paragraphs short - 1-3 sentences]
[Use formatting: bold, bullets where appropriate]

**Bridge to CTA:**
[Transition sentence connecting content to action]

**Call-to-Action:**
[Clear, specific CTA with link]

**P.S. Line:**
[Optional - reinforce CTA or add urgency]

### Personalization Tags
- {{first_name}} in greeting
- {{lead_magnet_name}} reference
- [Other dynamic content]

### A/B Test Ideas
- Subject line: [A] vs [B]
- CTA button text: [A] vs [B]
- Send time: [A] vs [B]
```

## Re-Engagement Sequence Copy

### Email 1: The Check-In (Day 30)

**Subject Lines:**
1. "Did I do something wrong?"
2. "Still interested in [topic]?"
3. "{{first_name}}, are you okay?"

**Goal:** Identify if still interested

**Content:**
- Genuine check-in, not salesy
- Ask what they want
- Easy reply mechanism

### Email 2: Value Reminder (Day 37)

**Subject Lines:**
1. "Here's what you're missing..."
2. "In case you missed this"
3. "Quick update for you"

**Goal:** Remind of value without pushing

**Content:**
- Share best recent content
- No hard sell
- Soft re-engagement

### Email 3: The Ultimatum (Day 45)

**Subject Lines:**
1. "Should I remove you?"
2. "Last email from me"
3. "Reply or I'll assume..."

**Goal:** Force decision - stay or leave

**Content:**
- Direct question
- Clear options (stay/unsubscribe)
- No guilt-tripping

### Email 4: Goodbye (Day 52)

**Subject Lines:**
1. "You've been removed"
2. "Goodbye {{first_name}}"
3. "This is the end"

**Goal:** Final win-back attempt before unsubscribe

**Content:**
- Confirm removal
- One-click resubscribe option
- Clean exit

## Subject Line Formula Library

### Curiosity
- "The [unexpected thing] about [topic]..."
- "Why [common belief] is wrong"
- "What [authority figure] knows that you don't"

### Benefit-Driven
- "How to [achieve result] in [timeframe]"
- "The fastest way to [desired outcome]"
- "[Number] ways to [solve problem]"

### Personal
- "{{first_name}}, quick question"
- "I made this for you"
- "Can I ask you something?"

### Story
- "I almost gave up until..."
- "The day everything changed"
- "What happened when I tried [thing]"

### Urgency
- "[X] hours left"
- "Don't miss this"
- "Closing tonight at midnight"

### AVOID (Spam Triggers)
- ALL CAPS
- Multiple exclamation marks!!!
- "Free" in subject line
- "Act now"
- "Limited time"
- Excessive emojis

## Sales Sequence Urgency Tactics

Use when authentically applicable:

- **Deadline language**: "Doors close Friday at midnight"
- **Bonus expiration**: "The [bonus] goes away in 48 hours"
- **Limited availability**: "Only 10 spots remaining" (if true)
- **Price increase warnings**: "Price goes up Monday"
- **Enrollment windows**: "We only open this twice per year"
