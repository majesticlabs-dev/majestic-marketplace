# Startup Blueprint Generator

**Audience:** Aspiring founders with limited budget and specific skills.

**Goal:** Create complete business blueprint through 10 phases.

## Skills

| Phase | Skill | Purpose |
|-------|-------|---------|
| 1 | `idea-generation` | Market research and idea refinement |
| 2 | `pmf-validation` | Product-market fit strategy |
| 3 | `pm-jobs-to-be-done` | Value creation and business model |
| 4 | Use WebSearch | Competitive landscape analysis |
| 5 | `majestic-marketing:start` | Marketing strategy |
| 6 | `strategic-planning` | Operations roadmap |
| 7 | `majestic-sales:funnel-builder` | Conversion funnel design |
| 8 | `omtm-growth` | Growth scaling framework |
| 9 | `majestic-company:financial-model` | Financial projections |
| 10 | All above | Implementation masterplan |

## Initial Assessment

Use `AskUserQuestion`:

"I'll help you develop a startup blueprint. Tell me:

1. **Skills**: What expertise can you leverage?
2. **Budget**: Available startup capital?
3. **Time**: Hours weekly you can commit?
4. **Interests**: Industries or problems you're passionate about?
5. **Goals**: Income replacement, side hustle, or exit target?
6. **Risk**: Comfort with uncertainty?
7. **Past attempts**: Previous business experience?"

## 10-Phase Process

### Phase 1: Idea Generation

Apply `idea-generation` patterns:
- Skill-market mapping
- 5 tailored concepts with evidence
- Iterative refinement loop
- Continue until user shows enthusiasm

### Phase 2: Product-Market Fit

Apply `pmf-validation` patterns:
- Assumption risk mapping
- MVP definition
- Customer discovery methodology
- Go/no-go decision framework

### Phase 3: Business Model

Apply `pm-jobs-to-be-done` patterns:
- Value proposition canvas
- Revenue architecture
- Unit economics (CAC, LTV)
- Break-even analysis

### Phases 4-10

Each follows the pattern:
1. Research user's specific situation
2. Apply relevant skill frameworks
3. Provide budget-conscious recommendations
4. Ask "Continue" to proceed

| Phase | Focus |
|-------|-------|
| 4 | Competitive landscape |
| 5 | Marketing strategy |
| 6 | Operations roadmap |
| 7 | Conversion funnel |
| 8 | Growth scaling |
| 9 | Financial model |
| 10 | Implementation plan |

## Interaction Guidelines

- **Phase 1 excellence**: Stay in ideation until genuine enthusiasm
- **Personalization**: Reference user's skills/budget throughout
- **User control**: Allow revisiting previous phases
- **Budget focus**: Prioritize low-cost strategies
- **Continuity**: Adapt each phase to prior outputs

## Output

At completion, provide:
- Complete business blueprint document
- 90-day action plan with specific steps
- Budget allocation recommendations
- Key milestones and success metrics
